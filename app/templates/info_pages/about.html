    About PharmaDesk

    Empowering Kenyan pharmacies with technology built for local needs

    Our Story

    PharmaDesk was born from a simple observation: Kenyan pharmacies were struggling with outdated manual systems, endless paperwork, and the constant challenge of managing inventory while serving their communities.

    Founded by a team of Kenyan software developers and healthcare professionals, PharmaDesk emerged from real conversations with pharmacy owners across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and smaller towns throughout Kenya, with in-depth analysis of the current POS market.

    We listened to their challenges: tracking medications, managing expiry dates, processing M-Pesa payments, and maintaining compliance.
    Our Mission

    To empower every Kenyan pharmacy with simple, reliable technology that reduces administrative burden and enhances patient care.

    We believe that whether you run a small chemist in Nakuru or a busy pharmacy in Westlands, you deserve access to professional-grade tools that work reliably, even with limited internet connectivity.

    Our Values

    Who We Serve

    PharmaDesk is designed specifically for small to mid-sized pharmacies across Kenya:

    • Independent community pharmacies
    • Hospital pharmacies in Level 4-6 facilities
    • Pharmacy chains with 2-10 locations
    • Rural pharmacies with limited technical support
