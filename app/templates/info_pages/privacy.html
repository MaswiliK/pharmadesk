    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 12, 2025 | Compliant with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019

    Introduction

    At PharmaDesk, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019.

    We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of all data entrusted to us by Kenyan pharmacies.
    Information We Collect

    • Pharmacy name and physical address
    • Primary contact person's details
    • Email address and phone number
    • Pharmacy and Poisons Board license

    • Inventory information
    • Sales transaction records
    • Customer purchase history
    • Supplier information

    • IP addresses and device information
    • Browser and OS details
    • Usage patterns and performance data
    Data Security & Usage
    How We Use Your Information
    • Providing PharmaDesk services
    • Processing transactions
    • Sending system notifications
    • Improving our software
    Security Measures

    256-bit SSL encryption

    AES-256 encryption

    MFA protection

    Daily backups

    M-Pesa Transactions

    PharmaDesk integrates with M-Pesa for payment processing but does not store M-Pesa transaction details. All payments are processed directly through Safaricom's secure systems.

    We only retain transaction confirmation numbers for reconciliation purposes.
