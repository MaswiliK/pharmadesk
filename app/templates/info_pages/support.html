    Support & Help Center

    Quick answers and assistance for PharmaDesk users

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I reset my password?

    Click on "Recover Access" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.

    How do I record a sale?

    Navigate to the Sales section, enter the medication name (with live search), enter the quantity, select payment method (Cash or M-Pesa), and click "Process payment". The system will automatically update inventory and generate a receipt.

    For M-Pesa payments, ensure you have stable internet connection
    Can I process M-Pesa payments offline?

    PharmaDesk requires internet connectivity for M-Pesa transactions to ensure secure payment processing and real-time confirmation.

    How do I set up low stock alerts?

    Go to Inventory Settings, select each medication, and set your minimum stock threshold (default is 5 units). The system will automatically alert you when stock falls below this level.

    What happens when medicines expire?

    PharmaDesk tracks expiry dates and sends alerts 30 days before expiration. Expired items are automatically flagged in red and cannot be sold through the system.

    Expired medications should be removed from shelves as per Pharmacy and Poisons Board regulations
